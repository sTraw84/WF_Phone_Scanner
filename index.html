Warframe Relic Scanner

Tip: The example image above is for quickly scanning relics for fissure missions.
You can also upload your own screenshots of your relic inventory for either the 4 relic (Fissure) or Mass Scan options.
When the mission begins, load the options menu and take a picture of the users in the top left to capture their relic data or manually enter the relics.
Prices may take a moment to load after scanning.
Notes:
  • This app is hosted on a free backend server. If the server has been inactive, it may take up to a minute to start up on your first request.
  • Mass scans may take longer to process and display results, especially for large numbers of relics.
  • Performing mass scans can sometimes exceed the server's request limits, resulting in errors such as "Too Many Requests", "N/A", or "No Listing Found". If this happens, you can manually re-enter any relics that failed to return results.
